Sylvia Ratnasamy Receives 2015 Grace Murray Hopper Award

Apr 21, 2015

Congratulations to Sylvia on this prestigious award!

The Grace Murray Hopper Award recognizes the outstanding young computer professional of the year, selected on the basis of a single recent major technical or service contribution.

Sylvia was recognized "for her seminal work on distributed hash tables, a fundamental contribution to the design of large-scale distributed and decentralized computing systems." Read her ACM profile here. Congratulations!
